Our Company

Welcome to Shanghai Aleksan Architectural Design Co., Ltd. Based in Shanghai and established in 2020, we are an architectural design studio dedicated to delivering innovative, functional, and contemporary design solutions. With a strong commitment to quality and creativity, we aim to create spaces that respond to the needs of modern living and the dynamics of the urban environment.

Emmanuel Aleksanyan CEO & Architect Designer
